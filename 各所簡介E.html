醫護研究所 Institute of Medical and Nursing Care

Objectives

To stimulate the quality and efficiency of medical and nursing care in the Asian region through mutual visits, researches presentations of papers, and exchanges of information.

Plans and Implementation
  • Exchange of study groups, experts etc.
  • Special research projects on medical and nursing care on retainer from other organizations or institutions.
  • Sponsorship of topic specific research seminars, workshops, symposiums, and forums in the Asia Pacific region.
  • Engaging in comparative research on issues of the medical and nursing care of different countries or territories.
  • Publishing periodicals on medical and nursing issues in the Asia Pacific region to promote and enhance joint development of quality health care.

人文暨社會研究所 Institute of Humanities and Social Science

Objectives

To raise the living standards of cultural and social stability in Kaohsiung region and to connect the modern technology with the civil culture intimately. So that both of them will be aided and profited mutually, creating and balancing the permanent happiness of mankind and to promote the development of social culture in the fields of society politics, art and all industries, making Kaohsiung Region become the hub of Asia Pacific Cultural Center.
